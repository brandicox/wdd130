Overview

Purpose

Dry Oar White Water Rafting Company is a small, family-owned business that offers white water rafting trips on the Pigeon River in Tennessee. We are passionate about providing our customers with an unforgettable and safe rafting experience. We believe that everyone should have the opportunity to experience the thrill of white water rafting, regardless of their experience level.

Our website will be a valuable tool for us to reach new customers and promote our business. It will also serve as a resource for our existing customers to learn more about our company, our trips, and the Pigeon River.

The following are some of the specific goals that we hope to achieve with our website:

  • Increase brand awareness: We want to make people aware of Dry Oar White Water Rafting Company and the unique rafting experiences that we offer.
  • Generate leads: We want to use our website to attract potential customers and encourage them to book a rafting trip with us.
  • Provide information: We want to use our website to educate potential customers about white water rafting and the Pigeon River.
  • Build relationships: We want to use our website to connect with our customers and build relationships with them.

Audience

The target customers for Dry Oar White Water Rafting Company are active, adventurous adults and families who are looking for a fun and challenging outdoor experience. They are typically between the ages of 25 and 55, and they have a moderate to high income. They are interested in spending time outdoors and trying new things.

Here is a more detailed description of the target customers:

  • Life situation: They are typically active people who enjoy spending time outdoors. They may have children, but they are still able to get away for weekend adventures.
  • Age: 25-55
  • Financial situation: Moderate to high income
  • Interests: Outdoor activities, adventure travel, new experiences
  • Wants: They want to have a fun and challenging outdoor experience that they can remember for years to come.
  • Needs that aren't being met: They may not have the time or experience to plan and execute a white water rafting trip on their own. They may also be looking for a more personalized experience than what is offered by larger rafting companies.

The target customers will usually access the Dry Oar website through search engines or social media via their computers, laptops, or mobile devices. They may also learn about the company through word-of-mouth or by seeing advertising in local publications.

When the target customers visit the Dry Oar website, they will be looking for information about the company's rafting trips, including the different types of trips offered, the difficulty levels, and the prices. They will also be looking for reviews from other customers and photos and videos from past trips.

The Dry Oar website should be designed to be informative and easy to use. It should include clear and concise descriptions of the company's rafting trips, as well as a booking system that is easy to navigate. The website should also include high-quality photos and videos from past trips, as well as testimonials from satisfied customers.

By understanding the target customers' needs and wants, Dry Oar White Water Rafting Company can create a website that is both informative and persuasive. This will help the company to attract new customers and book more rafting trips.
